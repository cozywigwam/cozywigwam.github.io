Welcome

Sunflower Sea Star is my technical hub. Although I'm a bit bird-crazy, the sunflower sea star seemed like a fun analogy. The domain name was available, too, which surprised me, given that gems like phalacrocorax.com are already taken. Cozywigwam (GitHub, Twitter) is a handle.

Polyambidextrous is the umbrella site. Sinistrocular, The Anti-Authority (just a 404 at the moment, but a future dictionary), and Cyan-eyed Vireo have assorted combinations of social media and/or a website. I like keeping them separate for various reasons, not least of which is the opportunity to explore different technical avenues.
